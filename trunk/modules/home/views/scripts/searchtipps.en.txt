<ol>
<li>Person`s names should be entered this way: Last name, First name</li>
<li>It is possible to substitute single unknown characters of a term by _ (underline). For example: Ma_er in search field person provides different ways of spelling (e.g. Maier, Mayer etc.).</li>
<li>Some search fields refer to authority files. Please consult them when in doubt before searching the fields <a href="/fak_liste.php" target="_blank">Faculty</a>, <a href="/inst_liste.php" target="_blank">Institute</a> or <a href="/dokart_liste.php" target="_blank">Document type</a>.</li>
<li>The field <b>Free text</b> searches <i>title</i>, <i>keyword</i> and <i>abstract</i> at the same time.</li>
<li></li>
<li>Use the search field <b>Source</b> to search for parallel publications (e.g. in a journal, a conference issue etc.) of documents.</li>
</ol>
