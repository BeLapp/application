The author can choose, which terms of use must be regarded for his/her publication. These terms are defined 
in a license.<br/><br/>
A number of different licensing models are available; you can find an overview at the institute for legal 
advices on free and open source software (IfrOSS) on 
<a href="http://www.ifross.de/ifross_html/lizenzcenter-en.html" target="_blank">http://www.ifross.de/ifross_html/lizenzcenter-en.html</a>. 
The model of the Creative Commons Lizenz should be emphazized here; with this model you can create your own 
license text covering all of your needs. You can create your personal license on 
<a href="http://creativecommons.org/license/" target="_blank">http://creativecommons.org/license/</a>. 
<br/><br/>
On publications from a professional publisher mostly the publisher specifies the license agreements and they 
are fixed in the contract or at least the license agreement is influenced by the contract.
<br/><br/>
This repository supports different, from which you can choose the one, that is most appropriate to your idea.
