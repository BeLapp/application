You can decide to whom your publication should be available. All document types except for doctoral thesises can be assigned to certain ranges of people, others are not allowed to see these documents. You can define ranges of IP-addresses or certain single addresses or you can use a password to protect your documents. If you don`t select something in this form your document will be published completely free.<br/><br/>
You should use this feature when there are some legal concerns, which could require a publication not completely free or if you have some clauses in a contract with someone preventing a completely free publication. Basically all publications on this repository should be free and published under an open-access-license.
